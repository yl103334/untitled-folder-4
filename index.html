About Me

Introduction

My name is Yerui Luo, though some may know me as Yuris Luo. I am a Digital Artist currently residing in New York, and my work spans various mediums and techniques. With five years of focus on digital art, I have fostered a deep understanding of its complexities. Additionally, I've spent three years studying and practicing more traditional methods such as oil painting, sketching, and color pencil art.

Or you might know me as Garlic Head, 爆香蒜头

My expertise isn't restricted to the arts; I've also spent a year gaining programming knowledge, with an emphasis on Java and Python. This has allowed me to specialize in the field of Interactive Media art, where I can blend my artistic and technological skills. Despite the contrasting nature of these disciplines, I find that they often intersect in ways that enhance and add depth to my work.
